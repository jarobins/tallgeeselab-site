Responsible disclosure

Reporting a security issue

If you believe you’ve found a security issue related to Tall Geese Labs assets, please report it responsibly. This page is a lightweight starting point and may evolve.

How to report

  • Email: security@tallgeeselabs.com (or contact@tallgeeselabs.com)
  • Include: affected URL/system, steps to reproduce, impact, and any proof-of-concept details
  • Please do not access data that isn’t yours or degrade service availability

Guidelines

Good faith testing

Minimal necessary testing to demonstrate the issue—avoid privacy violations and disruption.

No extortion

No threats, demands, or ransom language. Those reports will be ignored and may be referred to authorities.
